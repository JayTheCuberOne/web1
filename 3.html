1. 2x2

2x2 is the smallest puzzle in cubing. It is also one of the easiest, too. It has over 3 million combinations! Isn't it quite interesting? One of the best solvers in the world solved it less than a second. It is really unbelieveable!

2. 3x3

3x3 is the most classic puzzle in cubing. It is most well-known Rubik's Cube. It has over 43 quintillion combinations and it might be hard to solve it for some of you. Most begginers start with this puzzle and solve it in a minute. The world record of this puzzle is 3 seconds.

3. 4x4

4x4 is a bigger puzzle than 3x3. It is also little bit more complicated to solve. You need to make centers, edges, and fix parities like OLL Parity and PLL Parity. It has more and more cominations than rest of these puzzles.
